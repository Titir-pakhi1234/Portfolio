Information About me

I have done my MSc in Physics from St. Xaviers College Kolkata and BSc from Ashutosh College. I joined IIT Guwahati as a PhD scholar in July 2021 under Dr Santabrata Das. I got the prestigious PMRF through direct entry channel in July 2021, cycle 7. My thesis work is based on Black Hole X-Ray binaries, where I am analyzing X-ray data from RXTE satellite. I am doing timing and spectral analysis such systems. My work is in collaboration with ISRO, where I am also supervised by Dr. Anuj Nandi. My Portfolio

I am actively engaged in a comprehensive research project centered on Black Hole X-ray Binaries, with a particular focus on analyzing RXTE data from the source XTE J1859+226. My work encompasses an in-depth examination of both spectral and timing properties, with a keen interest in unraveling the intricate dynamics of the accretion disk and understanding the connection to jet formation. I am also conducting a parallel investigation involving ω class observations of GRS 1915+105 using data from the AstroSat satellite, further enhancing my expertise in the field of high-energy astrophysics.
